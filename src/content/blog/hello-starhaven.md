Hi, I'm Patrick. I've been wanting a place for my projects and the occasional write-up that isn't a social-media account. This is that.

## What lives here

Outside of my day-to-day on Homebrew, three smaller projects are in flight:

- **[Brewy](https://github.com/starhaven-io/Brewy)**: a SwiftUI macOS GUI for Homebrew.
- **[macOSdb](https://macosdb.com)**: a catalog of which open-source component versions ship with each macOS and Xcode release.
- **[pinprick](https://pinprick.rs)**: a supply-chain security scanner for GitHub Actions.

## What to expect

Open source, security, and whatever else I feel like writing about. There's an [RSS feed](/rss.xml) if you prefer a reader.
